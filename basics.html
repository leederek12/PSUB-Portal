Basics

Here's what you'll need to know to use oueve!



Water tracking made easy.

Using one of our preset options or using the Oueve Assistant, you can add water and track it. With oueve, it's not a hassle to remember how much water you drank.


Track your progress.


Haven't been drinking a lot of water lately? Oueve is an iOS app that allows you to monitor your drinking habits in a single, unified space. You'll be able to tell if you need to drink more water easily, as all of that information will be in a single place.



Adding Water


Adding Water is easy with presets of commonly used containers. A slider is placed convinently for custom sizes.

Tracking Consumption


Tracking water conusmption is easy with a click of a button. All water consumption data is present and clear with the amount of water and size of container. Totals for monthly consumption and for daily consumption are also present on this screen.
