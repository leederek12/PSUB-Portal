    Hours

    Here's where you can record all of the hours you spend on the Purdue Student Union Board.

    "My Committee" points are all hours that are spent for your committee. Examples include: Committee Meetings, Board Meetings, required Physical Marketing, and events for your own committee.

    "Single Intercommittee" points are all hours spent at PSUB that are not for your committee. Examples include: Attending other committee events, Marketing hours outside of required slots, and work for other committees.

    "Double Intercommittee" points are all hours spent at PSUB that are for helping other committees hold their events.

    If you have any questions, feel free to ask the Vice President of Personnel.

    Here's where you will record your office hours.

    "Required Events" are used to record your events or events that you are required to go to.

    "Office Hours" are used to record hours spent during office hours.

    "Meetings" are used to record hours for meetings. Examples include: committee meetings, meetings w/ President, meetings w/ Advisor.

    If you have any questions, feel free to ask the President.
