Oueve Assistant

An Assistant that Works With You




Oueve Assistant is designed to provide detailed and statistical data at the touch of a button. Oueve Assistant recommends how much water you should drink to stay hydrated.




Forgot to record an entry? Don't fret, with Oueve Assistant you can add specific entries for any time.




Oueve Assistant can also delete specific entries with the search of size or date.





Want daily reminders or reminders in 3 hours? No problem. With Oueve Assistant, reminders can be easily set with a press of a button. Alter your recommendations by telling Oueve Assistant when you want reminders.





With its advanced tracking capabilities, Oueve Assistant can adapt to your daily agenda and your current location so you don't have to worry about staying hydrated.
