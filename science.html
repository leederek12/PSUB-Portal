Why Oueve?


Stay hydrated

Did you know that your body is composed of over 60% water? To maintain this healthy balance of fluids, Oueve helps keep track of your water consumption, so you'll know if you're on track.


Help your kidneys

Your body constantly needs water to flush out waste from your body. Oueve makes sure that you drink enough water to ensure that your body is working the way it should. Staying water also prevents kidney stones and increases waste absoption in kidneys.


Perform better

Your muscle tissue is composed of 75% water. Drinking water ensures that these muscles can perform at maximum capacity when you need them the most, on and off of the field. Drinking water aids with muscle recovery times and prevents cramps during crunch time.1
